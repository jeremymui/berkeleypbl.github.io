Internal Networking

While not a committee that takes applicants, the Internal Networking committee is responsible for fostering the social environment of the organization. From planning the semesterly Member's Retreat to organizing countless social events throughout the semester, the Internal Networking committee works hard to encourage a tight-knight PBL community.

Gallery

From ice skating and dodgeball to weekend outings to Santa Cruz or Lake Tahoe, our Internal Networking chairs prepare social events of all kinds throughout the semester.
